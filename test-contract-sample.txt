CONTENT MANAGEMENT AGREEMENT

This Content Management Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on December 15, 2024, between:

CONTENT CREATOR: Alex Rivera ("Creator")
MANAGING ENTITY: Starling Music Management LLC ("Manager")

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

1.1 The Manager agrees to provide comprehensive music content management including digital distribution across all major platforms, rights management, marketing campaigns, and revenue collection services.

1.2 Manager shall handle all aspects of content promotion including social media marketing, playlist placement, and brand partnership negotiations.

1.3 All services shall be performed in accordance with industry best practices and applicable laws.

2. FINANCIAL TERMS

2.1 Manager shall receive twenty percent (20%) of all gross revenues generated from the managed content.

2.2 Payment shall be made quarterly within thirty (30) days of the end of each calendar quarter.

2.3 Manager shall provide detailed accounting statements with each payment.

3. TERM AND TERMINATION

3.1 This Agreement shall continue for a period of three (3) years from the effective date.

3.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement with sixty (60) days written notice.

3.3 Upon termination, all rights shall revert to the Creator, except for ongoing obligations.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

4.1 Creator retains all intellectual property rights in the content.

4.2 Manager receives limited rights for management purposes only during the term of this Agreement.

4.3 Manager shall not use Creator's intellectual property for any purpose outside the scope of this Agreement.

5. GENERAL PROVISIONS

5.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

5.2 Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Los Angeles, California.

5.3 This Agreement may only be modified in writing signed by both parties.
